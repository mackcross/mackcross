Science Engagement in Rural Iowa

In 2016, I graduated from CAM High School, which is a consolidated school within rural Iowa. Unfortunately, rural schools such as the one I attended are under-funded and under-resourced. I work within my community to increase science engagement.

Visualizing Iowa Education

Using publicly available data from the Iowa Department of Education, Iowa State University, USDA, and other sources, I created a serious of maps to explore poverty, resource allocation and educational outcomes across Iowa counties

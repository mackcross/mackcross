Visualizing Iowa Education

Why is science engagement in rural Iowa critical? I created a series of maps to explore poverty, resource allocation and educational outcomes across Iowa counties. All data used in these maps was publicly available data and from sources such as Iowa Department of Education, Iowa State University, and USDA.
